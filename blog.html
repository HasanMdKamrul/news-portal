What is the difference between var,let and const?

All three of them used to declare variables but after the ES6 and ES6+ launched there is almost no use of var. let is used to declare variable which can be re-assigned later but not re-declare. On the other hand const means constant, which is you can declare and assign const together once but later no re-assigning and re-declare is possible. let and const are block scope and they don't hoist. In contrary, var can be re-declare and re-assign easily also can hoist thats why to avoid complications we don't use var very often.
